Community

A community is a social unit of any size that shares common values.
At JRS, we are involved in several communities and understand the power and importance of community. We are constantly striving to not only improve ourselves, but our communities as a whole. Our craft is constantly moving and the only way to keep up is to embrace the community and learn and share.
